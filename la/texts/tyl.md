# Teach Yourself Latin

## Unit 1

### 1.1. Grammar

> Brūtus Portiam [amat](../verbs/a/am/amo.md). *‘Brutus loves Portia.’*
>
> Brūtus Portiam minus amat. *‘Brutus loves Portia less.’*
>
> Brūtus Portiam nōn amat. *‘Brutus does not love Portia.’*
>
> Brūtus nōbilis *‘noble Brutus’*
>
> fīlia Brūtī *‘Brutus’ daughter’*
>
> pulchra Brūtī fīlia *‘Brutus’ beautiful daughter’*
>
> Brūtus in [hortō](../nouns/h/ho/hortus.md) cum Portiā est. *‘Brutus is in the garden with Portia.’*

### 1.2. Reading

> Ubi sunt nautae? *‘Where are the sailors?’*
