# Teach Yourself Latin

## Unit 1

### 1.1. Grammar

> [Brūtus](../nouns/b/br/Brutus.md) [Portiam](../nouns/p/po/Portia.md) [amat](../verbs/a/am/amo.md). *‘Brutus loves Portia.’*
>
> Brūtus Portiam [minus](../adverbs/m/mi/minus.md) amat. *‘Brutus loves Portia less.’*
>
> Brūtus Portiam [nōn](../adverbs/n/no/non.md) amat. *‘Brutus does not love Portia.’*
>
> Brūtus [nōbilis](../adjectives/n/no/nobilis.md) *‘the noble Brutus’*
>
> [fīlia](../nouns/f/fi/filia.md) [Brūtī](../nouns/b/br/Brutus.md) *‘Brutus’ daughter’*
>
> [pulchra](../adjectives/p/pu/pulcher.md) Brūtī fīlia *‘Brutus’ beautiful daughter’*
>
> Brūtus in [hortō](../nouns/h/ho/hortus.md) cum Portiā est. *‘Brutus is in the garden with Portia.’*
